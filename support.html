Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our support services

Do you guarantee scholarship awards?

No. We provide expert guidance, support, and resources to maximize your chances, but final selection decisions are made by scholarship committees and universities. Our role is to help you present the strongest possible application.

Do you write SOPs or CVs for students?

We provide professional templates, structure guidance, and review services, but we don't write documents from scratch. Our approach empowers you to create authentic, personalized documents while ensuring they meet professional standards.

Can I apply without IELTS or TOEFL?

It depends on the country, university, and specific scholarship requirements. Some programs offer English proficiency waivers or accept alternative proofs. We provide guidance based on your target institutions and available options.

What's your response time for queries?

We typically respond within 24-48 hours for email queries. Priority support is available for urgent deadlines. For complex consultations, we may schedule dedicated sessions to provide comprehensive guidance.

Are your services free or paid?

We offer both free guidance resources and premium personalized support services. Basic queries and template access are free, while dedicated mentorship, document reviews, and extensive consultations are offered through our premium plans.
